Who We Are

IIBSE (India International Board of Skills & Education) is an autonomous skills education board registered under Ministry of Corporate Affairs (Section 8 – Govt. of India). Our mission is to bring modern, flexible, industry-based learning for schools, colleges, and technical institutions.

Our Purpose

  • To establish a modern skill-based education framework
  • To affiliate schools & training institutes across India
  • To conduct examinations & issue certifications
  • To promote digital learning and automation
  • To reduce documentation burden & corruption in affiliation

Legal & Constitutional Base

IIBSE works under the rights granted by:
Article 19(1)(g), Article 29, Article 30, Article 14, Article 21A, Article 45, Article 46, Article 51A(h), and Article 300A.
