About IIBSE

IIBSE – Indian International Board of Skills Schools & Education is an autonomous skills and education board registered under Ministry of Corporate Affairs (Section 8). IIBSE works with schools from Nursery to 12th and institutions offering vocational & diploma programs, enabling them with modern digital learning tools, automation, transparent certification, and skills-based development.

Our Vision

  • Skill-based education for all schools from Nursery to 12th & Vocational Diploma Courses
  • Digital transformation of schools and institutions
  • Verified certification with full academic transparency
  • Empowering rural and urban education equally through technology

Our Mission

  • Develop modern learning modules and integrate them into every school
  • Provide 100% digital academic services and automation tools
  • Empower students with real-world skills and advanced learning outcomes
  • Expand nationwide access to skill-based schooling systems
