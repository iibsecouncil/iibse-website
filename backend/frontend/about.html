About IIBSE

IIBSE – Indian International Board of Skills Schools & Education & IIBSE Examination Council is an autonomous skills & education board, empowering schools from Nursery to 12th and institutions with modern digital learning solutions, transparent certification, and skill-based development.

Our Vision

  • Skill-based education for all schools from Nursery to 12th & Vocational Diploma Courses
  • Digital transformation of institutions
  • Verified certification and academic transparency
  • Helping rural and urban schools equally

Our Mission

  • Develop Nursery to 12th education in every village of India
  • Develop modern learning tools
  • Support schools with automation
  • Empower students with real skills
  • Expand nationwide academic reach
