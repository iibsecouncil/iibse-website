About IIBSE

IIBSE – Indian International Board of Skills Schools & Education is an autonomous skills & education board registered under the Ministry of Corporate Affairs (Section 8). IIBSE partners with schools from Nursery to 12th and vocational institutions, providing modern digital learning tools, automation support, transparent certification systems, and skills-based development.

Our Vision

  • Skill-based education from Nursery to 12th & Vocational Diploma Courses
  • Digital transformation of schools & institutions
  • Verified certification with academic transparency
  • Empowering rural & urban education equally

Our Mission

  • Develop modern learning modules for every school
  • Provide 100% digital academic automation
  • Empower students with real-world skills
  • Expand nationwide access to modern schooling systems
