Courses Offered

IIBSE Council offers a variety of skill-integrated and diploma courses for students after 12th and technical school students. All courses are aligned with NSQF and industry standards.

Diploma in Digital Skills

Learn coding, website design, AI basics, data analytics, and modern IT skills.

Healthcare Certification

Short-term and long-term paramedical courses for hospitals, clinics, and community care.

Technical Skill Development

Hands-on vocational courses in electronics, mechanical, IT, and industrial skills.

Professional Workshops

Soft skills, communication, management, and leadership training for employability.
