Academic Programmes & Skill Education (NEP 2020 + NSDC Aligned)

IIBSE follows the **National Education Policy 2020 (NEP 2020)** framework and **National Skills Qualification Framework (NSQF/NSDC)** standards to deliver high-quality academic and skill-based education from **Nursery to 12th**, along with **Diploma & Vocational programmes** after 10th and 12th.

NEP 2020 Academic Structure – 5+3+3+4 Model

Foundational Stage (5 Years: Nursery – Grade 2)

  • Nursery / LKG / UKG
  • Grade 1–2
  • Play-based learning
  • Basic numeracy & literacy
  • Foundational skills development

Preparatory Stage (3 Years: Grade 3–5)

  • Language, Maths, EVS, Art Integrated Learning
  • Introduction to basic computers
  • Foundational skill modules

Middle Stage (3 Years: Grade 6–8)

  • Science, Mathematics, Social Sciences
  • Skill-based modules (NSQF Level 1–2)
  • Vocational exposure & practical learning

Secondary Stage (4 Years: Grade 9–12)

  • Subject specialization
  • Skill modules mapped to NSQF Level 3–4
  • Board-level skill assessments

Skill Modules (Integrated from Class 6–12)

  • Basic Computer Applications
  • Entrepreneurship Skills
  • Digital Literacy
  • Retail and Business Skills
  • Health & Wellness
  • Arts / Craft / Fashion Basics
  • Vocational Foundation Programmes

Diploma Programmes After 10th (NSQF Aligned)

These programmes are mapped to NSQF Level 3–5 under skill sector councils.

  • Diploma in Medical Laboratory Technology (DMLT)
  • Diploma in ECG & Cardiac Care
  • Diploma in Nursing Assistant
  • Diploma in Early Childhood Care & Education
  • Diploma in Fashion Design & Garment Technology
  • Diploma in Office Management & Computer Skills
  • Diploma in Agriculture Technician
  • Diploma in Hospitality & Tourism Services

Diploma / Vocational Courses After 12th

Advanced skill programmes aligned with NSDC job roles and industry standards.

  • Advanced Diploma in Allied Health Sciences
  • Advanced Diploma in Radiology Assistantship
  • Advanced Diploma in Montessori & Teacher Education
  • Advanced Diploma in Fashion Designing & Boutique Management
  • Advanced Diploma in Computer Applications (ADCA)
  • Advanced Diploma in Business & Retail Management
  • Advanced Diploma in Hotel & Catering Services
  • Advanced Diploma in Agriculture & Rural Development

Short-Term Vocational Courses

  • Computer Basics & Digital Skills
  • Beautician & Makeup Artist
  • Basic Tailoring & Fashion Crafts
  • Youth Skill Development Programmes
  • Mobile Repairing / Electrical Technician Basics
  • Soft Skills & Personality Development
